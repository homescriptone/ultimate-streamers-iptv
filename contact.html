Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app come with channels?

No. Ultimate Streamers IPTV is a player only. You need to provide your own M3U playlists or Xtream Codes credentials from a service you subscribe to.

How do I add my playlist?

Open the app, navigate to Settings, and enter your M3U URL or Xtream Codes credentials. Your content will load automatically.

Is the app free?

A free tier with limited features is available. A premium subscription unlocks all features. Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

Can I get a refund?

Subscriptions are handled by the app store you purchased through. Please contact Apple or Google directly for refund requests.

How do I report a bug?

Send us an email at homescriptone@gmail.com with details about your device model, OS version, and a description of the issue. Screenshots are helpful too.
